Bhim Koirala

Born and brought up in Damphu, Chirang Bhutan, Mr. Koirala got his Primary education from Damphu Junior High school in Bhutan.He acquired high school education in Refugee camp schools in Nepal. He holds a bachelor’s degree in Business Management from Nepal. Mr. Koirala resettled in the US in August 2009. Bhim worked most recently as the Kentucky Office for Refugee’s Bhutanese Nepali Community Health Worker. He now works as a Global Health Navigator at the University of Louisville’s Global Health Initiative’s 550 Clinic.

Our Services

Bhutanese Society of Kentucky perform and demonstrate wide range of eevnts for community benifits.
Sports, Cultural events, Training and Public Awareness are main frontline events hosted.
Learn more about the events and project based work we do in community.

Sports

A week-long event will show cased 9-a-side soccer tournament among different teams within the Louisville Community. On the day of the Finals, BSK volunteers also design some awareness campaign to educate community at large and New Citizens Vote Awareness program with assistance from different resettlement agency. The event will also host Blind Hit and Musical Chair for adults and honored Senior-Citizens for their hard work and determination.


Culture

In addition to lively cultural songs and dances performed by the Bhutanese youth, the program also show cased the 5years Service Award to BSK volunteers, recognition of expresidents, calendar launch, college graduate recognition, volunteer of the year award and the 2015 annual report. The event will be generally attended by 300+ participants from Louisville, Lexington, and Bowling Green. (Event Sponsors in the past: Nick Bhatt, Bejoy Koshi, and Tek Rizal.)


Training

Bhutanese Society of Kentucky offers varieties of training to the community people to empower thier personal and and professional life. These training incliues not limited to mental health awareness training, leadership trainig, higher education litracy and health navigation training and many more. We also partner with different agencies and colaborate to bring some of high skills training and resources needed for the commuinity.


Awareness

Going back to our history and time we spent around 20 years as refugee, BSK frequently need awareness program design to the community people This includes but not limited to safe driving, scams, robbery and theft, voilence within the family , financial crisis etc. On monthly basis BSK partners/host awareness program and involve metro government for collaborations and suggestions from them to our community people.
