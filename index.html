Bhim Koirala

Born and brought up in Damphu, Chirang Bhutan, Mr. Koirala got his Primary education from Damphu Junior High school in Bhutan.He acquired high school education in Refugee camp schools in Nepal. He holds a bachelor’s degree in Business Management from Nepal. Mr. Koirala resettled in the US in August 2009. Bhim worked most recently as the Kentucky Office for Refugee’s Bhutanese Nepali Community Health Worker. He now works as a Global Health Navigator at the University of Louisville’s Global Health Initiative’s 550 Clinic.

Pushpa Dhakal

Pushpa Dhakal was born in Bhutan. He had his early and higher education from Nepal . Mr. Dhakal has earned bachelor’s degree in accounting from Southwest University, Indiana. Dhakal has been helping the community since he moved to the US, he officially joined BSK as a volunteer in 2014 and served as a board treasurer before he accepted the position of administrative assistant. On this tenure, he maintain and mobilize the financial records in most standard and ethical way. He is working on accounting field.

Chandra Dahal

Chandra Dahal was born in Dagana Bhutan. He is currently pursuing bachelor degree in Computer Engineering and Computer Science from University of Louisville, Kentucky. He is candidate of Master students effective from May 2017. Dahal always believe that through the knowledge of emerging technology and serving in the community with those invention and creativity is often passion and honesty. Mr. Dahal joined BSK as a volunteer in 2014 and served as a Board member and web-master. He holds the leadership at UofL.
